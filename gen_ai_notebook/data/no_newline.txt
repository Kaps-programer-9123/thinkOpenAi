Lena was a curious girl who loved exploring the woods behind her house every afternoon she would pack a small bag with snacks a notebook and her favorite compass one day she discovered a trail she had never seen before it led her to a hidden pond with crystal clear water and colorful fish swimming near the surface excited she began sketching what she saw in her notebook suddenly a small turtle climbed out of the water and looked at her as if it wanted to say something she laughed and said hello little one this became her secret spot a magical place she would return to again and again.
